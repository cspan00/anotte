Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I can’t eat gluten, dairy, meat, seafood, water, or air?

Answer

Our menus are ever changing, but with enough notice we can accommodate any and all dietary restrictions...yes even if you’re allergic to water. If you plan to join us for an aperitivo and have dietary restrictions, please contact us and we can make it work.

Q: How much does a private event cost?

Answer

For catered dinners the average cost per person is $65 for private parties of 20 or less. For larger events there is a minimum of $1000 depending on menu selection and the amount of people.

Q: How much notice do you need to plan a private event?

Answer

We prefer to have as much notice as possible but are able to put something together for you with a weeks notice. Always reach out and let us know what you need.
